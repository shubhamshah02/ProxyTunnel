01

Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the ProxyTunnel iOS application and this website. By using ProxyTunnel, you agree to the practices described here.

"We", "us", and "our" refers to Shubham Shah, the individual developer of ProxyTunnel.

02

Information We Collect

We collect nothing. ProxyTunnel does not transmit any personally identifiable information — no name, email address, IP address, or device identifier — to any server operated by us or on our behalf.

No account creation is required or possible. ProxyTunnel is fully functional with zero sign-in.

ℹ️
ProxyTunnel has no backend. There are no servers to receive your data even if we wanted them to.
03

Local Data Storage

All proxy configurations, saved profiles, preferences, and connection history are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and secure local databases). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

We have zero access to this data. It is encrypted at rest by iOS and protected by your device's biometric or passcode lock.

04

Network & Proxy Data

ProxyTunnel reads your device's current network environment and uses SSDP (Simple Service Discovery Protocol) to find proxy servers on your local network. This scanning is entirely on-device and local — no results are sent anywhere.

When you activate a proxy configuration, your device's system-level proxy settings are updated via iOS APIs. ProxyTunnel itself does not intercept, read, log, or transmit your network traffic.

⚠️
Third-party proxy servers: Any proxy server you connect to can see the traffic routed through it. ProxyTunnel only manages configuration — we are not responsible for the practices of proxy servers you choose to use.
05

Analytics & Tracking

ProxyTunnel contains no analytics SDKs. None. No Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Segment, no Crashlytics — nothing of that nature is included in the app binary.

We do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA). We do not build any behavioural profile. We do not sell or share data with any third parties.

✓
App Store Privacy nutrition label: ProxyTunnel's App Store listing reflects data-collection type "None" because that is genuinely true.
06

In-App Purchases

ProxyTunnel may offer optional Pro features via Apple's StoreKit framework. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We never receive, process, or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details.

Purchase history is managed by your Apple ID. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for how they handle payment-related data.

07

Children's Privacy

ProxyTunnel is a developer tool intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 — and since we collect no information at all, this is inherently satisfied.
